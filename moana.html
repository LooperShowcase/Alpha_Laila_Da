moana

Moana is a 2016 animated musical adventure film produced by Disney. The film is a huge success worldwide.

Movie story:

et in the Polynesian islands of the South Pacific, "Moana" tells the story of a brave girl named Moana Waialiki, daughter of the chief of the island of Motunui. Since childhood, Moana has felt the call of the sea, but her father forbids her from sailing for fear of her safety. When a mysterious curse strikes the island and its resources dry up, Moana decides to set sail alone to find a solution. Her mission is to return the heart of Te Fiti, the goddess of nature, which was stolen by the demigod Maui. During her journey, she meets Maui and embarks on challenging adventures with him.

Film themes:

Courage and Self-Discovery Respect for Tradition and Cultural Identity The Relationship Between Humans and Nature

Main Characters:

Moana: A brave girl who loves the sea and tries to save her people. Maui: A vain but kind demigod who wields a magical hook that allows him to transform into animals. Te Fiti: A nature goddess whose heart was stolen, causing havoc on the land.
