Frozen is a 2013 Disney animated musical film based on the Hans Christian Andersen fairy tale "Frozen." The film tells the story of Elsa, a princess with magical powers that enable her to create snow and ice, but she cannot control them well. When she inadvertently reveals her powers, it causes the kingdom where she lives to freeze.

movie story:

The film follows the journey of her sister Anna, who, along with supporting characters like Kristoff and Olaf (a snowman), embarks on a quest to find Elsa and stop the eternal winter that has swept the kingdom. The film's key messages revolve around family love, courage, and self-acceptance. Its hit song, "Let It Go," has become one of the most famous songs in film history.

The film was a huge success, leading to the release of a sequel, Frozen II, in 2019, which continues Elsa and Anna's adventures as they discover more about Elsa's powers and past.
